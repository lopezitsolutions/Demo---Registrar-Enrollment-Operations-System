How It Works

Four steps from walk-in to enrolled

The same flow our registrar's office uses every enrollment period.

01

Get a queue number

Walk-in applicants take a ticket at the registrar counter — no appointment needed.

02

Submit requirements

Present your documents for initial checking against the requirements list.

03

Settle down payment

Pay your reservation fee and choose an installment schedule that fits your budget.

04

Get confirmed

Receive your official enrollment confirmation and class assignment.

Requirements

What to bring on enrollment day

Requirements are checked and tracked per applicant at the registrar counter.

  • Form 137 / Transcript of Records
  • PSA Birth Certificate
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • 2x2 ID Photos (2 copies)
  • TESDA Registration Form (vocational programs)

Missing a document?

You can still start enrollment with partial requirements. Our registrar's office tracks each document as pending, received, or verified, so you'll always know what's outstanding.
